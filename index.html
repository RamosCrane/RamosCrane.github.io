About Us

Ramos Crane Connect & Haul Services is not a crane operator or transport provider — we are a specialized connector. Acting as a middleman and business broker, we help certified crane companies find high-paying work by sourcing jobs and managing client connections. You handle the lifting; we handle the leads. Earn more while we take care of logistics and sales — all for just a 7% commission per completed job.

Our Services

Knuckle Boom Crane Lifting

Expert handling of complex lifting tasks in tight or challenging spaces using precision-controlled knuckle boom cranes. We match you with clients needing this specialized service.

Heavy Equipment Hauling

We connect certified haulers with construction companies, industrial clients, and municipalities needing reliable and timely transport for heavy machinery.

Container Moving

Whether it's job sites, ports, or yards, we refer you to customers needing container loading, transport, and relocation handled efficiently.

Hazmat Transport

We refer qualified Hazmat-certified operators to businesses with compliant hazardous material transportation needs.

Oversize Load Escort Services

Ensure safety and compliance with our escort vehicle referrals for oversize loads. We help coordinate pilot vehicles for both short- and long-haul oversized transport.

Emergency Job Dispatch

We’re available 24/7 to match you with urgent job requests when clients need immediate crane or haul assistance. Stay ready and get notified fast.
